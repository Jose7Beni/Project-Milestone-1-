Topic 2: Cybersecurity in FinTech

Cybersecurity is critical in FinTech because financial platforms handle personal data, bank information, and payment activity that attackers may try to steal or misuse.

Why Security Matters

FinTech systems must protect customer accounts, transaction records, payment information, and business operations. Weak security can lead to fraud, data breaches, service downtime, and loss of trust.

Major Security Risks

  • Phishing attacks that steal usernames and passwords
  • Malware and ransomware targeting financial systems
  • Identity theft and account takeover
  • Data breaches involving customer information

Security Practices

  1. Use strong passwords and multi-factor authentication
  2. Encrypt sensitive financial data
  3. Monitor network traffic for suspicious activity
  4. Train employees and users to detect scams
