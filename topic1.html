Topic 1: Digital Payments

Digital payments are one of the most visible parts of FinTech. They let people and businesses transfer money electronically without using cash.

What Are Digital Payments?

Digital payments include online card payments, mobile wallets, bank transfers, peer-to-peer payment apps, and contactless systems. They are popular because they are fast, convenient, and easy to use in stores and online.

Common Examples

  • Mobile wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Peer-to-peer apps like Cash App and PayPal
  • Debit and credit card transactions
  • Online checkout systems for e-commerce

Digital Payment Comparison

Payment Method Example Main Benefit
Mobile Wallet Apple Pay Fast and contactless
Peer-to-Peer App PayPal Quick money transfers
Online Card Payment Visa Checkout Easy online purchases
